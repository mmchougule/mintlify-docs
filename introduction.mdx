`b402-solana` is a confidential DeFi layer for Solana. Shield USDC
into a ZK pool, then swap, lend, or open a perp through any
registered adapter — atomically, in one transaction — without your
wallet appearing on the post-shield tx. Each downstream protocol
records you as a PDA derived from a private spending key, not as a
wallet. That last sentence is the design choice that lets this work
on SVM. The trust posture is in
[Trust assumptions](/concepts/trust-assumptions); the cryptography is
standard Groth16-over-BN254 with a single-contributor Phase-2 today.

## What it looks like on chain

A `privateSwap` (Jupiter route + Kamino lend, **one transaction**) on
mainnet:

```
5aVtM9Vc3eSgakTwv8vNG2snokQCWZhc8D6vfLTYigJRTkW2eZ8Ymv5gYawD81qCfAyj3KzhvHJqvGA1znpLKUhB
```

[solscan](https://solscan.io/tx/5aVtM9Vc3eSgakTwv8vNG2snokQCWZhc8D6vfLTYigJRTkW2eZ8Ymv5gYawD81qCfAyj3KzhvHJqvGA1znpLKUhB)
· `solana confirm 5aVtM9Vc… -u mainnet-beta -v`

Trace it: a relayer pubkey paid the fee, routed through Jupiter, then
landed in Kamino. The user who triggered the swap doesn't appear in
any account slot. **That's the property the proof binds.** Most
privacy projects can't show this on chain because they're testnet —
this composes Jupiter and Kamino in one shielded transaction, today,
on Solana mainnet.

## What's shipped

| Surface | Where |
|---|---|
| Shield / unshield | mainnet |
| Private swap (Jupiter V6) | mainnet |
| Private lend / redeem (Kamino, per-user Obligation) | mainnet |
| Private perp open / close (percolator) | devnet |

Multi-output adapters (whirlpool LP, two-asset unwinds), async
adapters (Jupiter Perps), and NFT-position adapters need the next ABI
revision — designed in PRDs, not yet in code.

<Note>
  **Mainnet beta.** Pool, verifiers, and adapters are deployed under
  Apache 2.0. Trusted setup is single-contributor today; multi-party
  ceremony and external audit are the next hardening steps. Full risk
  framing in [Trust assumptions](/concepts/trust-assumptions).
</Note>
